24 Hours Services

Everest Hospital provides comprehensive emergency services 24/7 with experienced doctors and state-of-the-art facilities. Our emergency room is prepared to deal with a wide variety of urgent medical situations, such as heart attacks, strokes, accidents, and more. Patients are given care in a timely and effective manner, with priority given to those whose ailments are life-threatening. In addition to this, the hospital offers a well-coordinated ambulance service that allows for the quick transfer of patients to the emergency department

Experienced Doctor

Our hospital's commitment to providing the highest level of medical care to our patients is reflected in our access to experienced and highly qualified doctors. Our medical team is comprised of individuals who have years of experience in their respective fields and possess a deep understanding of the latest medical advancements and treatments. With this expertise, they work closely with patients to understand their unique needs, develop personalized care plans, and ensure the best possible outcomes, utilizing the latest diagnostic tools and technologies and staying up-to-date on the latest research.

In the heart of the City

Due to the fact that our facility is situated within the city itself, we are able to provide you with timely medical assistance without the added strain of having to travel significant distances in the event of an unexpected emergency. Our devoted staff of medical specialists is always ready to give care of the highest possible standard.
