Welcome to Everest Hospital

A hospital, as a place where hope is born and miracles occur, should provide quality healthcare to all people, not just the privileged ones. Everest Hospital, conveniently located at the heart of the city, is committed to offering the highest level of integrated healthcare to the common man at an affordable price. In addition, we offer various discounted offers and support programs to assist those who are facing financial difficulties. Our experienced physicians and medical team utilize state-of-the-art equipment to provide you with top-quality healthcare services. Whether you require routine check-ups or are facing complex medical issues, our team is always available to offer the care and support you need to achieve optimal health and well-being. Because we take a comprehensive and holistic approach to healthcare, we offer a wide variety of medical services, such as emergency care, surgical services, women's health services, pediatrics and children's health services, cancer care, hea rt and vascular services, neurology, radiology, and imaging services, physical therapy and rehabilitation, laboratory services, pain management, sleep medicine, outpatient services, behavioral health services, and senior care. Furthermore, our warm and welcoming environment allows our patients to feel at ease when receiving medical attention. Our team is readily available to answer any questions or concerns you may have, which is an integral aspect of the care we provide. Therefore, we strive to make every patient's experience at Everest Hospital a positive and memorable one, as we believe that every patient deserves the best possible medical care.

Why to choose Everest?

  • Equal treatment for everyone; special discounted prices for impoverished and deprived people.
